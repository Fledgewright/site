Building Apps That Build You

Most apps are built to keep you. "Engagement", the industry calls it, and it runs on your attention and time. Fledgewright is a small studio building things that strengthen the interior and relational work of a real life, and then hand you your time back. Each app is designed to be needed less as you use it. No dependency hooks and no shame loops.

We make our own apps, sold once and owned forever, each one built for the slow work of a real life and designed to matter less to you over time. See the apps.

We also build for coaches and practitioners whose values line up with ours, turning their programs and workbooks into apps their clients can hold and grow from. One rule holds on every commission: the user wins. How commissions work.

If you'd like to collaborate, or just learn more, we would love to hear from you at hello@fledgewright.com.
