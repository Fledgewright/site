Building Apps That Build You

Most apps are built to keep you. "Engagement", the industry calls it, and it runs on your attention and time. Fledgewright is a small studio building things that strengthen the interior and relational work of a real life, and then hand you your time back. Each app is designed to be needed less as you use it. No dependency hooks and no shame loops.

What we've built

Two Apps, One Promise

The Practice in Our Preach

A CBT companion app · in closed beta on Google Play

Built on a life coach's cognitive behavioral workbook, it teaches you to catch the negative patterns affecting your life and correct them in real time, so that in time you can do it without the app. See more

Keeping the People Who Matter Close

A private, on-device memory keeper · public beta expected late 2026

A private, on-device second brain for neurodivergent minds. It holds the people and moments you care about, and when a connection has gone quiet it gently brings you back to what matters. See more
