Privacy

The short version: this site collects almost nothing, and what our apps hold belongs to you.

This Website

Nothing here asks you to sign in, and nothing watches you read. There are no cookies and no analytics. We host our own fonts, so your visit doesn't call out to a font service or any other third party. We run no ads and have nothing to retarget you with.

The site is served by GitHub Pages. Like most hosts, GitHub may log basic connection information, such as IP addresses, for security purposes. That logging is theirs, and we never see or use it. GitHub's own privacy statement covers it.

If we ever add visit counting, it will be an aggregate, cookieless count that cannot identify you, and this page will describe it before it runs.

When You Write to Us

Mail to hello@fledgewright.com is ordinary email. We keep the correspondence so we can reply and remember what we talked about. Writing to us doesn't put you on a mailing list, and we don't share your address with anyone.

Inside the Apps

Each app explains its own data practices in plain language, where you get it and inside the app itself. These rules hold across everything we build:

  • We collect the minimum needed to deliver the benefit, and we can justify every field we keep.
  • You can export your data, and you can delete it.
  • Your personal information and your relational history belong to you.
  • We do not sell user data, and our business is not built on exploiting it.
  • In a commissioned app, your interior work stays yours. By default a coach sees presence and progress, never content. Anything more requires consent you grant knowingly and can take back at any time, from your own side.

Questions

If anything here is unclear, or you want to know something this page doesn't answer, write to hello@fledgewright.com.
