Build Your Work into a Tool Clients Can Hold

If you have a workbook or method that genuinely helps people, we can build it into an app your clients hold in their hands. Your name and lineage stay on the work, and you share in how it does. Whether it's sold or given freely is your call.

We also take on ideas. If there's an app you've dreamt existed and never been able to find or build, we'd like to hear about it.

Every commission starts with a conversation about what the tool should teach and where it should end. We build things people finish with, and we're careful about whose work we take on. The practical details, from privacy to revenue, get worked out there, in the open, before anything is built.

If that sounds like how you'd want your work carried, write to hello@fledgewright.com and tell us about it.
