Who, or What, is a Fledgewright?

A playwright makes plays. A shipwright builds ships.
A wright is simply a maker of things.

To fledge is to grow your flight feathers and leave the nest for good.
Every bird starts its adult life as a fledgling.

A Fledgewright is a maker of things that help you fly away from them in the end.
Graduation is the craft.

We stay in the background. The apps carry their own names, and we carry the idea underneath them. You never have to know we exist to be helped by what we made.

Most software measures success in time spent. We measure it in time you get back. A feature earns its place only if it leaves you more capable on your own, and every workflow has a point where you're done.

When a coach pays for an app a client uses, their interests can pull apart. We set the rule before any money is on the table. The user always wins. We sell our own apps once, to own. We would rather earn once from a tool you outgrow than forever from one you can't leave.
