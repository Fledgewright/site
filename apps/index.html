The Apps

We build apps in two ways. The first is our own ideas, sold once and owned forever, to anyone who has need of them. The second is commissioned work from coaches, built for their client base and theirs to sell or give freely. Both paths carry the same promise: nothing built to hook you, and a clear point where you're finished.

The Practice in Our Preach

A CBT companion app · in closed beta on Google Play

Our first completed app was built on a life coach's cognitive behavioral, mood, and self-esteem workbook, a program designed for the slow work of trusting yourself again. Like the program, the app teaches you to spot the negative patterns affecting your life and correct them in real time, so that in time you can do it without the app. It's accepting applications now. Full release and price still to come, with an iPhone version later. Want an invite? Write to hello@fledgewright.com.

Keeping the People Who Matter Close

A private, on-device memory keeper · public beta expected late 2026

An in-house concept: a private, on-device second brain for neurodivergent minds, built primarily for AuDHD. It holds the people and moments you care about, documented by you rather than found on their social media, and when a connection has gone quiet it gently brings you back to what matters. Everything stays on your device, with no accounts and no data farming. Just your memories, kept safe and reflected back to you.
